   The end of September and the beginning of October every year is the most worthwhile time to go to Northern Xinjiang to see the scenery. The autumn wind is getting colder, and the snow-capped mountains, golden wooden houses, and the white birch forest in Hemu Village are turning into the clouds. The autumn colors of the small village are particularly brilliant. The dreamy scenery of smoke and mist makes people feel like they are in another world, like a beautiful fairy tale world.

Hemu Village

Autumn is the best time to travel to Xinjiang. The leaves of the autumn grass trees turn golden yellow, and the forests are dyed in many colors, forming the most beautiful pastoral picture. When we arrived at the viewing platform, we dismounted and took photos. The beautiful scenery in front of us made us feel very good. This snow-covered Hemu Village is hidden in the northwest border and deep in the Altai Mountains of northern Xinjiang. It has preserved the original natural environment and the original living customs of the Mongolian Tuva people for hundreds of years.
